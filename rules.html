RULES

The rules for the Pheonix MC network and our Discord.

Global Rules

These rules apply to the entirety of Pheonix. Including the Discord and Minecraft Network


#1 Alternate accounts are allowed
#1.1 A maximum of 5 alternate accounts are allowed.
#1.2 Using an alternate account to bypass a punishment is not allowed.
#1.3 Using open source/free alternate accounts is not allowed.
#1.4 Sharing an account is allowed, but the owner is responsible for all actions taken on the account.

#2 Blacklisted modifications are not allowed.
#2.1 Hacked clients are not allowed.
#2.2 Any modification that is not on the allowed modifications page is not allowed.
#2.3 Any modifications that give players an unfair advantage is not allowed.

#3 Any threats/threating is not allowed.
#3.1 Death threats are not allowed.
#3.2 DDOS/Hack threats are not allowed.
#3.3 Dox threats are not allowed.
#3.4 Suicide encouragement is not allowed.

#4 Discrimination/Disrespectful remarks are not permitted
#4.1 Sexual remarks are not allowed.
#4.2 Homophobic remarks are not allowed
#4.3 Racisit remarks are not allowed
#4.4 Any kind of harassment is not allowed.
#4.5 Staff or Player disrespect is not allowed.
#4.6 Dating/Date-like roleplaying/Inimate messaging is not allowed.

#5 Exploitation of any Bugs or Glitches is not allowed.

#6 Exsessive Chat disruption is not allowed.
#6.1 Message Spam is not allowed.
#6.2 Character Spam is not allowed.
#6.3 Caps lock is not allowed.
#6.4 Bypassing the Swear Filter/Swearing/Using Swear Abbreviations is not allowed.
#6.5 Promoting exsessive chat disruption is not allowed.

#7 Any non-DarkSide related content is not allowed.
#7.1 Any mention of other Discord or Minecraft server is not allowed.
#7.2 Any links that are not of DarkSide, or any unrelated links are not allowed.
#7.3 Advertising or mentioning your own or other YouTubers/Twitch Streamers is not allowed.

#8 Any type of impersonation is not allowed.
#8.1 Impersonating a YouTuber/Streamer is not allowed.
#8.2 Impersonating a Company/Corporation is not allowed.
#8.3 Impersonating a Player or Staff Member is not allowed.
#8.3 Impersonating a Player or Staff Member is not allowed.

#9 Any sort-of Greifing is not allowed. This includes Plots and Islands.
#9.1 Scamming is not allowed. Nor is IRL trading.
